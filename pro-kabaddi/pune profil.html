PROFILE 

Puneri Paltan


Owned and managed by Insurekot Sports Pvt. Ltd.,
Puneri Paltan is one of two Pro kabaddi league
franchiese from Maharashtra. The team represents
the city of pune, comining the magnificence of
Maharashtra's cultural capital with the true flavours
of this indigenous sports. Insurekot Sports Pvt. Ltd.
is passionate about Kabaddi and committed to 
promoting the sports and works with an aim to 
create awerness around kabaddi's rich heritage in
the country. The team masterminded by Ashok 
Shinde is lead by Wazir Singh.

Recreating the glory of the game's rich history in
India, Puneri Paltan unleshes the energy and
fortitude of kabaddi in it's morden day version
