Following the huge success of the inaugural edition of Pro Kabaddi League (PKL), the professional Indian sport marks it return with the second season. Pro Kabaddi League 2015 kicks off on July 18, Saturday with the first leg is scheduled to be hosted in Mumbai. PKL 2015 is expected to follow the monstrous success and positive response of previous year with big names like superstar Amitabh Bachchan and Salman Khan promoting the tournament. The complete timetable of PKL featuring 60 games till final on August 23 played across 37 days has been listed. According to match fixture, defending champions Jaipur Pink Panthers begin the title quest in season opening match. Also: Pro Kabaddi League 2015 schedule: All match fixtures and complete time table with free live streaming of PKL15. Pro Kabaddi League received huge applauds in its last season, where it was promoted by various Bollywood celebrities including Salman Khan and Abhishek Bachchan. Abhishek Bachchan’s Jaipur Pink Panthers were the winners of the first season. They won this title after a tense game against Mumbai. According to the match fixture, these two teams will be competing again in the first match of this season at the National Sports Club of India (NSCI) in Mumbai. Legendary actor Amitabh Bachchan has already raised the anticipation by crooning the song of the tournament – Le Panga. Also: Pro Kabaddi League 2015: Amitabh Bachchan sings a song titled ‘Le Panga’ . The eight teams participating are – Bengal Warriors, Bengaluru Bulls, Jaipur Pink Panthers, Puneri Paltan, Patna Pirates, Telugu Titans and U Mumbai will be traveling to their home grounds in this season which will end on August 23rd, in Mumbai. Pro Kabaddi League, which was widely viewed in its opening season will once again be telecast by Star India. They will broadcast the second season in five languages – Hindi, Telegu, English, Kannada and Marathi – across eight channels.
